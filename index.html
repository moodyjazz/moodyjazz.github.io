  9. Word of the Year 12/5/24
    min's notes

    A fun one inspired by Oxford's WOTY. Similar to the Pantone Color of the Year, I don't think these X of the Year things are scientifically backed up or anything, but rather a reflection of the times according to a small group of semi-qualified people. Whatever. Still fun. This puzzle was inspired by the everchanging language of Gen Z, though I'd argue there is a culture difference between Old Gen Z (e.g. me at 26) and Young Gen Z (e.g. my brother at 21).
